A humble potato is cooked in the humblest of ways.

Ingredients

  • potato
  • water
  • salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a pan with water
  2. Place potato into pan.
  3. This next step is for those who like flavour, add salt to the water.
  4. Turn the stove on and allow the water to boil.
  5. Once water has reached boiling, turn the heat down to simmer.
  6. Allow the potatoes to cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Drain potatoes and enjoy yourself a boiled potato.
